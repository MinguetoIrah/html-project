Always Remember To:

PROPER ATTIRE

Wear appropriate workout clothing and closed-toe athletic shoes. No jeans, sandals, or street clothes allowed. This ensures safety and hygiene while using equipment and participating in classes.

NO CELL PHONE CALLS

Avoid loud phone conversations in workout areas. Use your phone for music or fitness apps only. This maintains a focused environment for all gym-goers.

NO FOOD IN WORKOUT AREAS

Consume food only in designated areas like the nutrition bar. Keep workout spaces clean and odor-free. Water in closed containers is allowed.
