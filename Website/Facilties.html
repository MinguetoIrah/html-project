Our Facilities

Modern Workout Equipment

MODERN WORKOUT EQUIPMENT: ISM Fitness Gym boasts cutting-edge exercise machines and free weights. Our equipment is regularly updated to provide the latest in fitness technology, ensuring effective workouts for all muscle groups and fitness levels.

Spacious Cardio Area

SPACIOUS CARDIO AREA: Our expansive cardio section features a variety of machines like treadmills, ellipticals, and stationary bikes. There's always equipment available, even during peak hours, allowing members to maintain their heart health comfortably.

Group Fitness Classes

GROUP FITNESS CLASSES: We offer diverse group classes led by experienced instructors. From high-energy Zumba to calming yoga, our classes cater to different interests and fitness levels, fostering a motivating community atmosphere.

Personal Training Sessions

PERSONAL TRAING SESSIONS: Our certified personal trainers provide one-on-one guidance tailored to your specific goals. They create customized workout plans, offer nutritional advice, and provide the motivation and accountability needed to achieve optimal results.

Outdoor Trainig Area

OUTDOOR TRAINING AREA: A versatile outdoor space for bootcamp-style classes, yoga sessions, and functional training. It allows members to enjoy fresh air while working out.
